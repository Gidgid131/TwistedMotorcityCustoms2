4" Handlebar Risers Old School

$100-$120

Upgrade your motorcycle's style and comfort with our Old School Handlebar Risers. These risers feature a classic design that hides the top two bolts, leaving only a single, smaller bolt visible in the center. This creates a uniform look that complements the vintage style of your motorcycle.

Our Old School Handlebar Risers have a 4-inch rise and are designed to fit 1-inch handlebars. They come with all the necessary bolts included, including 1/2" x 20 mounting bolts, 5/16"-18 top bolts, and a 3/8"-24 cap bolt.

Please note that these handlebar risers are not recommended for racing applications, as they are made from softer metals, such as Aluminum, Brass, Bronze, Copper, or Nordic Gold, which are more prone to rust. To combat this, we apply a clear coat of protective wax to the surface of the part, but it is still recommended to keep these parts clean.
